WorkPay Helper Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Developer: KonWorksLab

App: WorkPay Helper

Package name: com.konworkslab.tools.workpayhelper

1. Overview

WorkPay Helper is a utility app designed to help users record work hours, workplaces, shifts, and estimated pay.

The app is intended for personal record keeping and pay estimation.

2. Information Stored by the App

WorkPay Helper may store the following information locally on the user's device:

  • Workplace name
  • Hourly pay rate
  • Work date
  • Clock-in time
  • Clock-out time
  • Break minutes
  • Work records and calculated values
  • App settings such as language, theme, and premium pay settings

3. Local Storage

Work records are stored locally on the user's device.

WorkPay Helper does not require an account and does not upload work records to a developer server.

4. Exported Files

The app may allow users to export work records as CSV files.

When a user exports or shares a CSV file, the user controls where that file is saved or shared.

5. Data Collection

WorkPay Helper does not collect personal data through a developer server.

The app does not currently include account login, cloud sync, advertising, analytics, or developer-operated data upload features.

6. Third-Party Services

WorkPay Helper does not currently use third-party advertising or analytics services.

If third-party services are added in the future, this privacy policy will be updated before release of that version.

7. Estimated Pay Disclaimer

Pay calculations in WorkPay Helper are estimates for reference only.

Actual pay may differ depending on employer policy, taxes, deductions, tips, overtime rules, local labor laws, and employment conditions.

WorkPay Helper does not provide legal, tax, accounting, or payroll advice.

8. Children's Privacy

WorkPay Helper is not designed for children.

9. Data Deletion

Users can delete work records within the app.

Users may also remove exported CSV files from the location where they saved them.

11. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as the app changes.
